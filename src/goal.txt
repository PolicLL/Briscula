What should this program do ?

Simulate card game Briscula.

THREE OPTIONS

It is possible to choose two options, play 1v1, 3 players in round and 2v2 where two people are playing against another two.

There is an admin who is responsible for dealing cards and calculating points and deciding who won.

HAVE OTHER PLAYERS AND PLAY BY YOURSELF

- You should simulate other people playing and also make ability to play.
- Make opponents "smart" in some level


BONUS

- You could simulate game using threads.
